# Medical Equipment Training

MediOpto provides comprehensive training programs to ensure healthcare professionals can maximize the potential of our optical equipment.

## Training Programs

### Basic Equipment Operation
- Equipment setup and calibration
- Basic maintenance procedures
- Safety protocols and best practices
- Troubleshooting common issues

### Advanced Operation Techniques
- Advanced imaging techniques
- Specialized procedure training
- System optimization
- Data management and analysis

### Certification Programs
- Equipment operation certification
- Advanced user certification
- Train-the-trainer programs
- Continuing education credits

## Training Formats

- **On-site Training**
  - Hands-on equipment training
  - Customized to your facility
  - Group training sessions

- **Virtual Training**
  - Live online sessions
  - Interactive webinars
  - Self-paced modules

- **Workshop Sessions**
  - Intensive hands-on practice
  - Small group settings
  - Expert-led demonstrations
